Assignment 3.T1 - Creating a Character with character.ai

In this hands-on assignment, we’ll get first-hand experience with generative AI by using character.ai to create an original character, and then collaborate with it to generate a short story excerpt.
You can find the site at https://beta.character.ai/

Guidelines:

  • Use character.ai to create a new character based on an archetype or existing character from mythology, folklore, literature or other fiction. Get creative but keep in mind any key attributes, background, and personality traits of that archetype.

  • Engage your AI character in conversation to develop a scene setup with a setting, situation, other characters etc. Prompt the character to begin telling a story from their perspective.

  • Let the story unfold through back-and-forth conversation. Feel free to steer the direction but allow your AI character’s voice to come through. Go for an excerpt about 250-500 words long.

  • optionally, you can further refine the story by taking the AI-generated draft and editing it yourself. But the key is collaborating with your AI character to write the initial draft.

Goals:

  • Get hands-on experience using character.ai for generative text
  • Think creatively about persona and story development
  • Observe how AI both demonstrates coherent generation but may also deviate in unexpected ways
  • Reflect on the collaborative creative process with an AI assistant

Let’s start bringing our imaginations to life with AI! Please share your character and story excerpt when complete.
